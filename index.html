This is Rocio, and I'd like to tell you about my Journey on this IT world.

How it started?

I remember myself presenting my first project at high school, It was a small bus ticket purchase program written in C. My teacher asked why I had decided to create that program, and from that day on I knew that I wanted to help people by making their lives easier.

Do you remember Sun Microsystems?

I do, they opened an Academy at my Collage, I was part of the first batch learning about Java. Nowadays if you ask around who created the Java programming lenguaje most of them will tell you that was Oracle. This makes me feel old sometimes.

I am still proud of had participated in ACM programming contests, I had a lot of fun with my colleages. Traveling to different states in Mexico and meeting more people that liked to code too.

My First Job

I started on migrating programs from Visual Basic to Java. Can you believe it?.

The Challenge

After years of working for Mexican companies I decided was time to move to the next level.

Living in Texas

My current employer gave me the opportunity to work side by side with my customer and since 2019 I'm living in Texas. I'ts been such as an experience. I have to mention that my favorite dish is homemade Texan-styled-Brisket

Working with proud

In the past few months my team started migrating functionality to Amazon Web Services, Is now that I realized how much I've been experiencing.

Well, I'll finish with this:

You never get bored on this IT world, always learning new Technologies, new Challenges, new problems to solve, new teammates, new ways to do the job.

And all of this to serve people, from people to people.
